About ALIV

One festival ground, many unforgettable moments.

ALIV is designed as a welcoming carnival destination with all-day and late-night experiences. From sunrise cultural showcases to midnight DJ sets, every day has a fresh reason to return.

Daily Programming

Live acts, dance circles, and cultural showcases every day.

Street Carnival Spirit

Color, costumes, drumming, and parade moments across zones.

Family-Friendly Options

Games, rides, and daytime activities for all age groups.

Food & Craft Village

Local flavors, grills, desserts, and artisan market stalls.

Festival Zones

Five worlds, one walkable carnival city.

Main Stage & Night Concerts

Headline performances, live bands, and crowd-powered singalongs.

Parade Street & Costume Lane

Masquerade groups, dance troupes, and colorful street processions.

Food, Play & Culture Village

Street food, games, art displays, and open community dance sessions.

Who It's For

A carnival for locals, travelers, and families.

  • Accra residents looking for a high-energy festive season experience.
  • Diaspora visitors planning memorable December holiday outings.
  • Families and friend groups wanting music, food, and safe fun in one place.

Whether you come for one evening or the full season, ALIV gives you a complete festival journey in one destination.
